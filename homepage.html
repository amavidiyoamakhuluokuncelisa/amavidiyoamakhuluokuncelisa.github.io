Ukwanda amavidiyo amakhulu okuncelisa

Amabele kubangele ingxoxo eyinkimbinkimbi mayelana nokuncelisa, isithombe somzimba, kanye nokusebenzisana kokuqukethwe ku-inthanethi. Abasekeli bathi la mavidiyo athuthukisa ukumelwa, anika amandla abesifazane abanezinhlobo ezifanayo zomzimba ukuze bazizwe beqinisekisiwe futhi beqiniseka ngokuhlangenwe nakho kwabo kokuncelisa.

Okuqukethwe okunjalo kungakhuthaza ukubandakanywa futhi kuqede imibono engavamile ezungeze ukuncelisa. Kodwa-ke, abagxeki baxwayisa ngezingozi ezingaba khona, njengokuqiniswa kwemibono engavamile kanye nokubekwa kokulindela okungenangqondo kokuncelisa, okungaholela emizweni yokungafaneleki.

Inkulumo igcizelela isidingo sezingxoxo ezinolwazi mayelana nokuncelisa kanye nesithombe somzimba, ikhuthaza ulwazi lwabezindaba kanye nenhlonipho ukukhuthaza indawo esekelayo kubo bonke abesifazane.
